Current Projects

Game Development

DeltaV is a game I've been working on since October of 2016. Inspired heavily by Kerbal Space Program, I wanted to create a real-time puzzle game game whose primary mechanic was executing impulsive maneuvers in a simiulation with realistic Newtonian physics.

This project also seems a perfect match for my art skills and style, of which I have none.

Brewing

Over Christmas I made a Blood Orange Hefeweizen. It was refreshing in our recent prolonged heat wave of a summer, and reviews from my friends were mixed.

As the weather cools down, I intend to make another batch of the ~9% American Brown Ale I first brewed late last year, containing half a dozen kinds of sugar, and unofficially dubbed "Molasses Beer".
